About

I am a programmer, angler, and gardener based in New York City. Currently, I work at Ramp, my usual lure is a TRD on an EWG Ned jighead, and I care for several cultivars of San Pedro cacti.

This site is built with Zola and served by Github Pages. The styling is written by myself, though it takes serious inspiration from the personal sites of Paco Coursey and Rasmus Andersson. The code is open-sourced on GitHub; you may do whatever you wish with it.
